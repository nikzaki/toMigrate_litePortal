Frequent Questions

Please find answers to common questions here, note we do our best to answer all questions our supporters may have.

  • What is myGolf?

    myGolf is a handy electronic scorecard that keeps all the scores for your golf rounds. In fact, you will never lose your scorecards again!

  • How do I find Tournaments?

    As soon as an Organizer posts a tournament in myGolf, all players will see it in on their myGolf mobile app. You can select any competition and register directly from your mobile, as long as the competition is 'open'.

  • Why should I use myGolf?

    Instead of jotting down the scores on a piece of card, the more efficient way is to record them in the myGolf app on your mobile phone. The scores are stored at www.mygolf2u.com and they are available at any time for analysis and improvement.

  • How do I download myGolf?

    There are 3 ways to use myGolf:
    1. Visit Google Play and search for mygolf2u
    2. Visit App Store and search for mygolf2u
    3. Simply go to m.mygolf2u.com and start using the app without downloading!

  • What are the minimum supported Operating System (OS) version?

    1. Android - Version 9.0 (Pie) and above [as of app version 4.3.x]
    2. iOS - 9.0 and above [as of app version 4.3.x]

  • What are the types of mobile phones that support myGolf?

    myGolf app is supported on any phone that runs Google Android and Apple IOS. In addition, you can simply a browser and login at m.mygolf2u.com on ANY phone.

  • How much does myGolf cost?

    Currently myGolf is a free of charge.

  • I have downloaded and installed, how do I register?

    If a friend has entered your details so that you can play in a round and have your scores stored, then you will have received an email with instructions on how to download and use myGolf. If you are downloading directly from Google Play or App Store, then start the app on your phone and click ‘Signup As New Member’. In the registration screen, fill in the requested details and then click on Signup. Only one time registration is required. You can also click here to register.

  • How do I contact myGolf?

    Feel free to drop us comments at mygolf2u@britesoftcorp.com
